# Typography Update Inspection Notes

The JNK Managing Group landing page was updated from the prior editorial type system to a more traditional pairing: **Libre Baskerville** for major headings/display moments and **Libre Franklin** for navigation, forms, body copy, and interface text. The updated hero, lead form, header navigation, and primary call-to-action were visually inspected in the live development preview.

The traditional serif heading treatment is visible in the hero headline and form heading, with stronger weight and slightly more conservative tracking/line-height to avoid the decorative feel of the previous font. Body copy and form labels remain legible, button spacing is preserved, and the client logo remains balanced in the fixed header. TypeScript and production build validation passed after the update.
